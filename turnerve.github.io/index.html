About Me

My name is Turner Van Erman, I am a highschool graduate from Angola, Indiana. Growing up, I was homeschooled, and I started to pursue basketball and guitar , but eventually I realized that I have more of a talent for coding. I have always been super compelled by computers and technology of just about any kind. I love fixing problems. I enjoy having the feeling of knowing that something you've put time and effort into is working just the way you want it to. I have recently graduated and fully completed the JavaScript accelerated learning 3 month program at Eleven Fifty Academy which is located at Launch Fishers in Fishers, Indiana. I was thrilled with my classes and my instructers, and just the experience as a whole. I love the team aspect of coding and solving problems. I am very interested in CSS and design aspects of coding in general. I have knowledge in JavaScript, HTML, AngularJS, MongoDB, Node, React, Redux, CSS, and jQuery. My goal for the future is to get a good job that I'll be excited to learn from and contribute to. I am continuing to learn more about JavaScript and some other languages on the side. I am not the type of person to just give up on something. From groundskeeping at Trine University, working at Rockin' Productions as a sales associate, or to working freight at my local Kohl's. I'm looking forward to what the future holds, and I'm constantly learning more every day, and loving everything life has to teach me.
